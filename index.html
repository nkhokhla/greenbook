Who we are?

Greenbook is an ecological foundation based in Lviv, Ukraine. Our activity takes place since October 2019. We think that sorting and recycling rubbish, planting trees and reducing one's carbon footprint should be involved into daily life of citizens - and we strive to achieve it. Our main business is selling e-books in cooperation with well-known shops. We strongly believe that only switching to electronic means of information completely can help us incredible rates of deforestation that occur today. When you buy a book in our store, 10% percent of your payment are spent on ecological initiatives - so you can be sure your money won't be wasted.

Our books are looking forward to meeting you.

Volunteer

Do you want to contribute to the improvement of quality of our lives? We give you an opportunity to do it. Our volunteers plant trees, collect rubbish in forests and on beaches, share cars and even hold educational speeches. We have neither special requirements, nor limitations based on position or age.
